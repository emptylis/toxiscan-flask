ABOUT US

ToxiScan is your personal food guardian, created by the passionate students of Amal Jyothi College of Engineering. Concerned about what's lurking in your groceries? Simply scan the ingredients, and our advanced technology will analyze them, letting you know if there's anything to worry about. With ToxiScan, you can make informed choices about what you eat, putting the power of knowledge at your fingertips.
